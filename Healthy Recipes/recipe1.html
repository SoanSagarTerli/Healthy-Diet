Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Estimated Calories: 250â€“300 per serving

Health Benefits:

  • Rich in plant-based protein and fiber.
  • Supports heart health with healthy fats from olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, tomatoes, onion, cucumber, and feta.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Sprinkle oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Let sit for 10 minutes before serving to allow flavors to blend.
  5. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
